Our core team

We all share the same mission: make tourism better. Our team is fun-loving, diverse, and we are young! We'd love for you to join our team, check out our careers page.

Better together

Noon Pakavaleetorn

Co-founder, General Manager

Noon is a purpose driven entrepreneur and a travel geek. She co-founded “Local Alike” with the belief that all travelers are also changemakers for the society in their own unique ways. She leads the overall mission and strategy to humanize tourism. She envisions tourism as a form of development tool that will lead to a more sustainable and inclusive society.

She is an expert in building relationships with locals and facilitating the social development projects with our partners.
