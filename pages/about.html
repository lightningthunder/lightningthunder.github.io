About us

Traveling is one of the best ways to see the world in new perspectives. We believe that traveling of any kind or the tourism sector as a whole must head towards more collective responsibility and sustainable outlook.

Our vision

Connecting you to local communities

Local Alike is an online marketplace where you can find local experiences organized by Thai communities. Instead of matching you to just one local guide, our curated experiences are designed and run by groups of locals who are enthusiastic about hosting travelers.
